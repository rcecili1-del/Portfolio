Professional Background

Robert Campos

BIM/VDC Professional

Introduction

I have 9 years of experience in the BIM/VDC industry and I am currently enrolled in the Master of Science in Construction and Facilities Engineering program at UNC Charlotte.

I work with project teams to turn design intent into coordinated building systems using BIM that support construction, commissioning, and long-term facility performance in complex environments such as process piping and highly coordinated MEP systems.

My experience spans pharmaceutical, healthcare, manufacturing, mission critical, education, multifamily, and public-sector projects, where I take a systems-driven, collaborative approach focused on clarity, reliable information, and data-rich models that support installation, turnover, and long-term facility use.

Professional Interests

My professional interests focus on BIM/VDC workflows, construction technology, artificial intelligence, and digital project delivery within the AEC industry. I am especially interested in how AI can support coordination, estimating, project management, and data-driven decision-making to improve construction efficiency and long-term facility operations.
